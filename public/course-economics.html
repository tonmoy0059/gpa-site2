Hi! I’m Riz Mohammad Hossain Khan

I’m proud to be the first Bangladeshi Silver Medalist and International Best in Finance at IEO 2022, and later Gold (3rd globally) at the International Business Olympiad 2023. I’m currently pursuing a BBA at IBA, University of Dhaka.

I focus on clear thinking and real-world cases, with an emphasis on the confidence to present your ideas under pressure. In my classes, you can expect practical frameworks, fast feedback, and a friendly yet competitive vibe.

What you'll learn

  • Micro and Macro Toolkits: You will learn to apply economic principles to real-world scenarios. For example, you might analyze how a change in interest rates affects consumer spending or how market structures impact pricing strategies.
  • Finance Essentials: You will master concepts like time value of money, budgeting, ratios, and valuation intuition. For instance, you could calculate the net present value of an investment or interpret a company’s financial statements to assess its health.
  • Game Theory and Behavioral Nudges: You will explore strategic decision-making and real-world behavioral insights. For example, you might model competition between companies or design incentives that influence consumer choices.
  • Data Handling: You will learn to interpret charts, identify trends, and distinguish causality from correlation. For instance, you could analyze sales data to predict future demand or evaluate policy impacts using economic indicators.
  • Case-Cracking and Pitch Craft: You will develop structured approaches to solving cases, creating slides, and delivering confident presentations. For example, you might prepare a pitch recommending a business strategy and defend it in a simulated boardroom Q&A.
  • IEO-Style Simulations: You will practice under Olympiad-style conditions with scoring rubrics and iterative coaching. For instance, you could complete a timed case challenge, receive feedback, and refine your solution for the next round.
