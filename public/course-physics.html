Hi! I'm Adnan Alamgir

I’m a two-time IOAA medalist—Bronze (2021) and Silver (2023)—and the 2024 Bangladesh Physics Olympiad National Champion. I graduated from Dhaka Residential Model College and am heading to Harvard University (Class of 2029).

I love making physics feel alive—from circuits to the cosmos—and turning curiosity into clear thinking. Expect patience, structure, and plenty of “aha!” moments.

If you enjoy questions as much as answers, you’ll feel right at home here.

  • Electricity & magnetism: circuits, fields, potentials
  • Waves & optics; thermodynamics & kinetic theory
  • Data analysis, experimental reasoning, graph reading
  • Olympiad tactics: modeling, estimation, time management
